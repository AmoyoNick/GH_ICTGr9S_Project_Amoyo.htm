Summary Fires can start anywhere and everywhere, especially here in the Philippines where we live in such a hot climate. Fires in this country are mostly caused by electronics such as electric fans being left plugged in, but in general, all electrics can start fires, and in here we will show you on how to avoid fires to keep you and your loved ones safe.
Always unplug your appliances when you don't need to use them, not only does this save electricity, but also does this save you and your family from potential fires.
Store your flammable items properly, and away from items that can probably cause a fire.
Be cautious around open flames, make sure that any flammable items are far from fires, and if you're planning to start your own campfire, make sure that you do it in a safe place.
If you use a gas stove at home, make sure that you dont have any gas leakage in your gas tank, and if you do smell gas, dont turn on any lights or any electronics. Turn off all electronics or kill the electricity in your entire house is possible using a circuit breaker. Open your windows to let the gas fumes leave your home, once done you can now safely use your electronics once again, and always make sure that your gas tank is properly sealed.
